About Us

GORWA (Goregaon Welfare Association) is a registered organization of concerned and involved citizens who have created this platform for citizens of Mumbai to raise their voice and be heard. This will require your active support, participation, knowledge and expertise to address civic and social issues and interact with an association of all Societies, NGOs, Community Groups and Institutes. We conducted several activities in this regard and received a very positive response from various social activists, citizen groups etc. Our prime objective is to encourage active participation of citizens in approaching and tackling civic issues and development work being undertaken with the involvement of MCGM / local authorities and elected representatives. Establish an Area Sabha based decision making process for local area development, keeping the common interest in mind, improve transparency and accountability.

GORWA Charity GORWA is a dedicated charity focused on empowering underprivileged students by providing essential support for NEET and JEE exam preparations. This organization aims to bridge the educational gap by offering resources, guidance, and financial assistance to help these students achieve their academic goals and secure a brighter future. Through their initiatives, GORWA ensures that talented and deserving students have the opportunity to succeed, regardless of their socio-economic background..

Start the new journey of life

Retirement is the beginning of another phase of life that can be satisfying and meaningful. At GORWA (Goregaon Welfare Association), we believe retired individuals have immense potential to contribute to society.

Our initiatives include mentoring, guiding seniors and youngsters, assisting small startups and NGOs, and fostering community engagement through various activities.

  • Mentoring and guiding seniors and less experienced individuals
  • Supporting small startups and NGOs
  • Sharing expertise through video conferencing and social media
  • Organizing regular gatherings and fun activities
