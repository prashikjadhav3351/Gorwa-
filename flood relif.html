The Flood Crisis in Chiplun

In Chiplun, Maharashtra, severe flooding caused by a surge in the waterfall led to the loss of essential educational materials for many students. GORWA swiftly mobilized resources and organized a distribution drive, providing new notebooks and textbooks to affected students. This intervention helped restore normalcy and showcased GORWA's dedication to supporting education, enabling students to continue their studies despite the disaster.

GORWA Provides Clothes to Chiplun Flood Victims

AAfter severe flooding in Chiplun, Maharashtra, GORWA quickly organized a drive to assist affected families who lost their belongings, including clothes. Within days, volunteers provided new garments for all ages, ensuring families could resume their daily lives with dignity. GORWA's swift action not only met immediate needs but also highlighted their commitment to holistic disaster support, restoring normalcy and hope to the community.
