GORWA (Goregaon Welfare Association)

GORWA is a registered organization of concerned and involved citizens who have created this platform for the citizens of Mumbai to raise their voice and be heard. This requires your active support, participation, knowledge, and expertise to address civic and social issues and interact with an association of all Societies, NGOs, Community Groups, and Institutes. We have conducted several activities in this regard and received a very positive response from various social activists, citizen groups, etc.

Our Prime Objective

To encourage active participation of citizens in approaching and tackling civic issues and development work being undertaken with the involvement of MCGM/local authorities and elected representatives. Establish an Area Sabha based decision-making process for local area development, keeping the common interest in mind, improving transparency and accountability.

GORWA Aims & Objectives

  • Encourage citizen participation in addressing civic issues and development work with MCGM/local authorities and elected representatives.
  • Solid Waste Management
  • Road & Footpath repairs and development
  • Footpath free of encroachment for citizens
  • Garden development and maintenance
  • Public Health Issues
  • Traffic Congestion related issues
  • Common area beautification
  • Promoting Green and Eco-friendly environment
  • Provide legal, consultancy, and administrative help to residents/societies for redevelopment, self-redevelopment, and SRA projects.
  • Provide consultancy and administrative help for services like Solid Waste Management, Wet Waste Composting, Dry Waste and E-Waste pickup, Rain-Water Harvesting, Solar Power Implementation, and assist in redressal of essential services like failure of electricity, water connection, etc.
  • Communicate with government authorities or take legal measures for the welfare of all residents for maintaining natural resources, getting amenities from the government & maintaining the same, security measures, etc.
  • Establish, manage and improve Parks, library, reading room, indoor and outdoor games and sports and recreational activities for the benefit of Goregaon residents.
  • Assist in maintaining & monitoring common areas such as Parks, Roads and water tanks, drainage, security systems, etc. and control mobile/cell phone towers setting.
  • Extend help and relief to residents of Goregaon in cases like ailments, accidents, hospitalization, demise, etc.
  • Create, promote and develop leadership qualities in youth, train them for social service, help the needy, and inculcate discipline and good moral character.
  • Promote Child development, Senior Citizens, and women welfare.
  • Conduct activities or things incidental or conducive to the running of GORWA in pursuance of its aims and objectives.
